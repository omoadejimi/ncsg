## Use numerals

Write numbers as figures, including 1 to 9: "You have 3 pending requests." Figures are faster to scan than words on a small screen, and they survive translation.

Exception: where a numeral could be misread at the start of a sentence, rewrite the sentence.

## Group long numbers

- Thousands separators always: **1,500,000**.
- Phone numbers in Nigerian reading groups: **0803 123 4567**.
- Account numbers in pairs or as the bank presents them: **0123 4567 89**.

## USSD codes

Write USSD codes exactly as dialled, in bold or code style, with no spaces: dial **\*737\*** or **\*565\*0#**. Always say what the code does beside it.

## Percentages and rates

Use the % symbol with no space: **2.5%**. For money, pair the rate with a concrete example — "1.5% fee (₦150 on a ₦10,000 transfer)" — because users reason in naira, not percentages.
