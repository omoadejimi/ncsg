## Use numerals

Write numbers as figures, including 1 to 9: "You have 3 pending requests." Figures are faster to scan than words on a small screen. They are also less likely to be mistranslated.

Exception: where a numeral could be misread at the start of a sentence, rewrite the sentence.

## Group long numbers

- Always separate your thousands: **1,500,000**.
- Phone numbers in Nigerian reading groups: **0803 123 4567**.
- Account numbers in pairs or as the bank presents them: **0123 4567 89**.

## USSD codes

Write USSD codes exactly as dialled, in bold or code style, with no spaces: dial **\*737\*** or **\*565\*0#**. Always say what the code does beside it. For example, 'Dial *123# to view your account balance.'
