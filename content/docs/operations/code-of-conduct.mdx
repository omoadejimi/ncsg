## Our standard

Everyone participating in NCSG spaces — issues, discussions, pull requests, events — agrees to:

- **Engage with ideas, not people.** Critique the guidance and the evidence; never the contributor.
- **Respect Nigeria's diversity.** No disparagement of any ethnicity, language, religion, region, gender or disability. Debates about language guidance must never become debates about whose language matters.
- **Assume good faith, especially with newcomers.** Many contributors are making their first open-source contribution.
- **Keep it honest.** Don't present opinion as research or misrepresent evidence.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, discriminatory remarks, personal attacks, sustained disruption, sharing others' private information, and retaliation against anyone who reports a concern.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report concerns privately to the editors at the contact address in the repository README. Reports are handled confidentially. Editors may warn, remove content, or ban participants, proportionate to the behaviour; enforcement decisions involving an editor exclude that editor.

## Scope

This code applies in all project spaces and when representing the project publicly.
