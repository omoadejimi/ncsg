## Lead with the news

The first five words decide whether a notification is read. Put the outcome first and details after.

<DoDont>
<Do>
₦50,000 received from Adaeze Nwosu. New balance: ₦126,400.
</Do>
<Dont>
Dear valued customer, this is to notify you that a credit transaction has occurred on your account.
</Dont>
</DoDont>

## Channel notes

### SMS

Still the most trusted channel for money in Nigeria — and the most constrained. Stay within 160 characters where possible, lead with the amount and direction (credit/debit), and include the balance only if the user opted in (SMS is visible on lock screens).

### Push

Title carries the news; body carries one detail. Don't duplicate the title in the body.

### In-app

Use for non-urgent information. Never block a money task with a marketing interstitial.

## Marketing vs transactional

Keep them separate in tone, channel and opt-out. A user who mutes "₦500 off airtime!" must never lose debit alerts as a side effect.
